NGIN#9 — LOGO SPEC (CONDENSED)

1. STRUCTURE
- Layout: Horizontal, centered block
- Format:
  NGIN#9
  ENGINE NUMBER NINE ———
- Aspect: 3:1 to 5:1

---

2. PRIMARY MARK (NGIN#9)
Font:
- Saira Stencil One (modified)

Style:
- Heavy stencil
- Hard edges (no rounding)

Treatment:
- Subtle distress (edge breakup, wear)
- Non-uniform fill (±5–10%)

Color:
- Base: #E6E1D8
- Range: #D8D2C8 → #F0ECE4

Rules:
- NO glow
- NO gradients
- NO shadows
- Texture must be baked

---

3. SUBTITLE (ENGINE NUMBER NINE)
Font:
- Altinn DIN (https://github.com/Altinn/altinn-din)
- Style: DIN-based geometric sans
- Monoline, mechanical

Weight:
- Regular (never bold)

Specs:
- Tracking: +150 to +250 (very wide)
- Size: ~15% of main text height
- All caps

Color:
- Core: #E3A72F
- Inner: #FFB84A
- Outer: #FF8C1A (low opacity)

---

4. GLOW (SUBTITLE ONLY)
- Inner radius: 2–4 px
- Outer radius: 6–12 px max
- Subtle, internal (embedded) feel

Rules:
- Slight uneven intensity allowed
- NEVER overpower main text

---

5. HORIZONTAL LINE
- Aligned to subtitle baseline
- Starts after text

Specs:
- Length: 1.5×–3× subtitle width
- Thickness: 1–3 px
- Same color + glow as subtitle

Imperfections:
- Minor breaks
- Slight taper
- 1–2 px misalignment allowed

---

6. SPACING
- Vertical gap: ~0.25× main text height
- Subtitle: LEFT-aligned to NGIN#9

---

7. DISTRESS (CRITICAL)
Type:
- Physical wear simulation (not overlay noise)

Include:
- Paint inconsistency
- Micro chipping
- Dirt/grime
- Vertical streaking

Rules:
- Directional (gravity-based)
- Non-repeating
- Subtle

---

8. COLOR ROLES
- NGIN#9: Physical marking (white)
- Subtitle: Active system (yellow)
- Line: Signal extension

---

9. RULES
DO:
- Maintain hierarchy (white dominates)
- Preserve wide tracking
- Keep distress subtle

DO NOT:
- Add symbols/icons
- Center subtitle independently
- Use modern effects (blur, gradients, gloss)
- Clean stencil edges
- Overuse glow

---

10. EXPORTS
1. Vector:
   - Black/white only
   - No texture, no glow

2. Print:
   - Texture baked
   - No glow

3. Screen:
   - Glow enabled
   - Texture included

4. Decal:
   - High distress
   - Alpha-ready

---

11. INTENT
Machine designation marking (not a brand logo)

Tone:
- Industrial
- Functional
- Controlled
- Non-decorative
